History

Is a company that works directly with small producers who have obtained organic certification under
fair trade conditions.The company has a processing plant located in the city of La Merced, Peru.
Chanchamayo Highland Coffee currently exports different types of coffee, cocoa, fruit juices, nectars, jams,
banana chips, dehydrated and candied fruits, and exotic liquors, all of organic status,
bound for France, Sweden, Finland, Denmark and Italy.
